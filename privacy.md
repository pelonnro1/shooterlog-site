# ShooterLog Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-06  
Version: 2026-03-06-v6

> **ShooterLog does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data with the developer.**

## 1. Scope

ShooterLog is a local-first app. In this build, data is stored on your device and there is no mandatory backend account sync.

You, as the app user, control your own session records. The app processes data only to provide local recordkeeping features.

## 2. Data We Process

- Session data you enter (date/time, place, event, guns, shots, notes, verifier name/signature).
- Images you add (target and gun photos).
- Technical metadata needed for app operation (for example timestamps).

## 3. Storage and Sharing

- Data is stored on your device.
- Backup files are created on your device and are shared only when you choose to share them.
- Restoring a backup replaces your current local data.
- If you store backups in third-party services (for example Google Drive, iCloud Drive, OneDrive), those services operate under their own terms.

## 4. Retention and Deletion

- Local data remains until you delete it.
- Backup files remain where you store them until you delete them.

## 5. Your Rights

Where applicable under GDPR, you may have rights to access, rectification, erasure, restriction, objection, and portability.

Privacy contact: [shooterlog.app@gmail.com](mailto:shooterlog.app@gmail.com)

## 6. Responsibility and Liability

- ShooterLog is a recordkeeping tool, not legal or firearms safety advice.
- You are solely responsible for lawful firearm/permit handling and for protecting backup files.
- To the maximum extent permitted by law, ShooterLog is provided "as is" without warranties.
