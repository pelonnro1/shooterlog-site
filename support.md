# ShooterLog Support

Need help with ShooterLog? Use one of the options below.

## Contact

- Email: `privacy@shooterlog.app`

## GitHub support channels

- Bug reports: https://github.com/pelonnro1/shooterlog-site/issues
- Questions and feedback: https://github.com/pelonnro1/shooterlog-site/issues

## Before you report

Please include:

- App version (from Settings/About)
- Device model and OS version
- What you were doing
- What you expected
- What happened instead
- Screenshots (if possible)

## Data and privacy notes

- ShooterLog stores data locally on your device.
- Backup/restore is user-managed through your chosen storage providers.
- Never upload sensitive personal files publicly in issues.
